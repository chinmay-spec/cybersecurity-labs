## Key Takeaways

- Enumeration is the foundation of penetration testing.
- Open ports reveal potential attack surfaces.
- Service versions help identify vulnerabilities.
- Network scans generate detectable patterns in logs.

---

## Defensive Insight

Repeated SYN scans or port sweeps may indicate reconnaissance activity and should be monitored in firewall and IDS logs.
