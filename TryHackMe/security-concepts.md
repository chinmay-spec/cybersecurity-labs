# TryHackMe – Security Concepts

## Objective

Understand cybersecurity roles and how offensive and defensive teams operate.

---

## Red Team vs Blue Team

* **Red Team** → Simulates attacks to find vulnerabilities
* **Blue Team** → Defends systems and monitors threats

---

## SOC Analyst Role

A Security Operations Center (SOC) analyst is responsible for:

* Monitoring security alerts
* Investigating suspicious activity
* Responding to incidents
* Escalating threats when necessary

---

## Alert Handling Process

* Identify suspicious activity
* Analyze logs and evidence
* Report the issue
* Escalate if required

---

## Key Takeaways

* Cybersecurity involves both attacking and defending systems
* SOC analysts play a key role in threat detection
* Proper alert handling is critical in real-world security environments
