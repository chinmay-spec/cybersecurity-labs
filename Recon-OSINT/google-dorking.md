# Google Dorking

## Objective
Use advanced search operators to discover publicly exposed information.

---

## Operators Practiced

- site:
- intitle:
- inurl:
- filetype:
- allintitle:
- allinurl:

---

## Example Use Cases

- Discover exposed PDF documents
- Identify public login portals
- Search indexed configuration files

---

## Security Risk

Improperly exposed documents may leak:
- Credentials
- Internal reports
- Sensitive data

---

## Mitigation

- Restrict directory indexing
- Use robots.txt properly
- Avoid exposing sensitive documents publicly
